# 🎨 ResearchIt v2.0 - Ink Wash Color Scheme

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors:
- **Charcoal Black**: `#1A1A1A` - Main background
- **Cool Gray**: `#808080` - Secondary text, labels
- **Soft Ivory**: `#F5F5F0` - Primary text, headings

### Accent Colors:
- **Gold**: `#D4AF37` - Primary accent, buttons, highlights
- **Bright Gold**: `#FFD700` - Hover states, emphasis
- **Silver Gray**: `#C0C0C0` - Secondary accent

### UI Element Colors:
- **Dark Gray**: `#2A2A2A` - Cards, inputs
- **Border Gray**: `#3A3A3A` - Borders, dividers
- **Hover Gray**: `#4A4A4A` - Hover borders
- **Light Gray**: `#A0A0A0` - Muted text

## Usage Guidelines

### Backgrounds:
- Main app: `linear-gradient(135deg, #1A1A1A 0%, #252525 50%, #1F1F1F 100%)`
- Sidebar: `linear-gradient(180deg, #141414 0%, #1A1A1A 100%)`
- Cards: `#2A2A2A` or `rgba(42, 42, 42, 0.8)`

### Text:
- Primary: `#F5F5F0`
- Secondary: `#C0C0C0`
- Tertiary: `#808080`
- Muted: `#A0A0A0`

### Accents:
- Primary buttons: `linear-gradient(135deg, #D4AF37 0%, #C9A636 100%)`
- Links: `#D4AF37`
- Highlights: `#FFD700`

### Borders:
- Default: `#3A3A3A`
- Hover: `#4A4A4A`
- Focus: `#D4AF37`
- Accent: `#D4AF37`

## Design Philosophy

This ink wash color scheme embodies:
- **Simplicity**: Clean, uncluttered aesthetic
- **High Contrast**: Excellent readability
- **Refined Balance**: Sophisticated and professional
- **Gallery-like Feel**: Perfect for showcasing research

Ideal for:
- Design portfolios
- Digital art platforms  
- High-end retail sites
- Academic research platforms

## Comparison to Previous Scheme

| Element | Old (Blue/Purple) | New (Ink Wash) |
|---------|-------------------|----------------|
| Background | Dark blue (#0f0f23) | Charcoal black (#1A1A1A) |
| Primary accent | Blue (#60a5fa) | Gold (#D4AF37) |
| Secondary accent | Purple (#a78bfa) | Cool gray (#C0C0C0) |
| Text | White (#ffffff) | Soft ivory (#F5F5F0) |
| Style | Vibrant, modern | Refined, gallery-like |
| Feel | Tech/gaming | Professional/academic |

## Benefits of New Scheme

1. **Higher Contrast**: Better readability and accessibility
2. **More Professional**: Suitable for academic presentations
3. **Timeless**: Won't feel dated quickly
4. **Sophisticated**: Conveys authority and expertise
5. **Unique**: Stands out from typical blue tech interfaces
